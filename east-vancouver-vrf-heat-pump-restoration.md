# How We Got Heat Back in an East Vancouver Apartment Building the Same Day

## The Short Version

An apartment building in East Vancouver lost heating across all units when their VRF system's main circuit board died. We showed up same-day, diagnosed it, ordered the board, and got their heat back before anyone had to spend a cold night.

## The Call

Property manager called. No heat anywhere in the building. Tenants were pissed.

The building runs on a central VRF heat pump — one system, whole building. When that thing goes down, nobody has heat. Not one unit. Not any.

In January in Vancouver, that's not an inconvenience. That's an emergency.

## What We Found

We arrived same-day. Checked the system. The main circuit board on the VRF unit was fried — no communication between the outdoor unit and any of the indoor air handlers. The whole system was dead.

No amount of resetting or tinkering was going to fix it. The board needed to go.
